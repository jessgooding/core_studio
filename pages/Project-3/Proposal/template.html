Proposal

My idea for the microinteractions based application is to have pages featuring a small collection of work from a few of my favorite photographers.

After swiping down from the title page (where I intend on having a microinteraction to make the transition smooth) there will be a page with a swiping library and two toggle switches beneath it.

These switches will be labeled 'Inverse' and 'Black & White' and when clicked the toggle will swap over and change color.
