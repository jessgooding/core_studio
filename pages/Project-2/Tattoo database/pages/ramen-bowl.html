How old are you? - 20 years old.

Where did you get it done? - Red Baron Studio by Vince C. Maximin (@vcmttt)

How impulsive was it? - Considered it for two months.
Does this tattoo have a meaning behind it? - I am deathly allergic to peanuts and have been hospitalized several times as a result. For every food that nearly kills me I said I would get a tattoo inspired by it. I got a bowl of noodles and it was topped with peanuts.
Is it your own artwork? - I drew this inspired by another tattoo I've seen. The noodles were spruced up by the artist but I added the egg.
