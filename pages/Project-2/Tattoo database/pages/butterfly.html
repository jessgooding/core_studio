TATTOO ONE
Name - Abbey Gregory
Tattoo and placement - Butterfly above the left elbow.
How old are you? - 19 years old.
How old were you when you got this tattoo? - I had just turned 18 like a week after.
How many tattoos do you have in total? - 3
How much did it cost you? - $300
Where did you get it done? - Sovereign Tattoo, Portland by artist @mothmilk.
How long did it take? - 2 and a half hours.
How impulsive was it? - Literally not impulsive at all. I think about each tattoo for at least a year before I get them.
Does this tattoo have a meaning behind it? - The first time I did shrooms with my best friend, on the come up I said what if we see butterflies of something when we start tripping. We were at a music festival and we started walking from our camp to the stage area and we saw a plastic butterfly on the ground. We both have a massive love for butterflies and both wanted to get tattoos. This is my first of three by this artist. Butterflies also represent personal transformation.
Is it your own artwork? - Art is by the tattoo artists.
